# Internet Sales System

A comprehensive sales tracking and management dashboard designed for internet service providers to monitor sales performance, customer visits, and team activities.

## Overview

The Internet Sales System is a web-based application built for Zakcom Internet Services that provides real-time analytics and insights into sales operations. The system helps sales managers and team members track performance metrics, manage customer interactions, and analyze sales trends over time.

## Features

### Dashboard Analytics
- **Sales & Visits Trend Tracking**: Visual representation of sales and customer visits over the last 30 days with line charts
- **Performance Metrics**: Real-time display of total sales, revenue, visits, and conversion rates
- **Sales Status Monitoring**: Track sales by their current status in the pipeline
- **Package Analysis**: Compare sales performance across different service packages with dual-axis charts showing both number of sales and revenue

### Customer Management
- **Visit Outcomes Tracking**: Pie chart visualization of visit results (Follow-up status)
- **Customer Objections Dashboard**: Monitor common customer concerns including:
  - Price Concerns
  - Coverage Concerns
  - Existing Provider Status
- **Customer Feedback System**: Dedicated section for collecting and reviewing customer feedback

### Team Performance
- **Top Performers Rankings**: Monthly leaderboard showing sales team performance with metrics:
  - Rank
  - Sales Person Name
  - Total Sales
  - Revenue Generated
  - Number of Visits
  - Conversion Rate
- **Team Performance Dashboard**: Overview of entire team activities and productivity
- **Individual Visit Tracking**: Detailed logs of all customer visits

### Sales Management
- **All Sales View**: Comprehensive list of all sales transactions
- **All Visits Log**: Complete history of customer interactions
- **Sales by Package**: Revenue and volume analysis by service package

### Navigation & User Interface
- **Intuitive Navigation Bar**: Quick access to:
  - Dashboard
  - Log Visit
  - New Sales
  - Visits History
  - Prospects Management
  - Team Overview
  - Users Management
  - Zakcom Portal
  - Logout
- **Clean, Modern Design**: Professional color-coded interface with responsive layouts
- **Card-Based Layout**: Easy-to-read metric cards with visual indicators

## Technology Stack

The system appears to use:

- **Charts/Visualization**: Chart.js or similar library for data visualization
- **Responsive Design**: Mobile-friendly interface
- **Color Scheme**: Professional blue, orange, green, and purple theme

## Key Metrics Tracked

1. **Total Sales**: Monthly and historical sales count
2. **Total Revenue**: Financial performance tracking in thousands
3. **Total Visits**: Customer interaction frequency
4. **Conversion Rate**: Percentage of visits converted to sales
5. **Sales by Status**: Pipeline management
6. **Visit Outcomes**: Success rate of customer meetings
7. **Customer Objections**: Common barriers to sales
